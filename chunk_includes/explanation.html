Hello, and thank you for your participation!

Amy, Bob and Chris are playing a card game to train their memory. Each round, they get to see a different card, but only for a few seconds. All cards consist of four cells, and each cell can contain one of five symbols: a circle, a triangle, a square, a star or a diamond.

The aim is to remember where certain symbols appeared on the card. Which symbols need to be remembered varies from round to round. If a player manages to recall the position of the relevant symbols correctly, she gets 2 points. If she makes a mistake, she loses 1 point. Each round, players also have the option to withdraw. If they withdraw, they won't win any points, but they won't lose any points either.

What you will see is the actual card, and how Amy, Bob and Chris remember it. Using this information, you will have to answer a question about Amy, Bob and Chris.

Here are some final recommendation before you start the experiment:

  • Always select all the correct answers before moving to the next item.
  • In some cases you might be unsure which answers to select. If this happens, follow your intuition!
  • If the checkboxes are too small (e.g., if you are using a tablet), you can click on the characters themselves.
  • The experiment will take about 15 minutes.
